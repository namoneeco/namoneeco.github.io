Hi, I’m Nico – a working sound designer on Vancouver Island, Canada.
                
            I developed my passion for digital audio during my undergrad in Sydney, Australia, where I lived for 4 years studying Philosophy & Religion. I began recording and mixing bands in small apartments, learning how to translate my ideas and express myself through sound through the computer.
            
            On return to Canada I began work as a FoH sound engineer for a live music venue. After several years of live audio work, I shifted focus to audiovisual media, working on videos and film.
            
            This path led me to games, where I have been using my experience in post production audio to building projects in Wwise, Unreal, and Unity over the past three years.
            
            I am self taught and I enjoy collaborating and problem-solving on technical projects.
