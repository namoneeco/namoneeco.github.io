Bio

Hi! I'm Nico, a sound designer living and working on Vancouver Island, Canada.

I got my start in digital audio during my undergrad in Sydney, Australia, where I studied Philosophy & Religion. I began recording bands and learning how to translate ideas into digital production.

Back in Canada, I worked as a FoH sound engineer before moving into audiovisual media and film post production.

My path has led me into game audio, where I build projects in Unreal, Wwise, and Unity.

I am self taught and enjoy collaborating and solving technical problems.
