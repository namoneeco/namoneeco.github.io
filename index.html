Hi! – I am a sound designer currently in Vancouver Island, Canada.
I developed my passion for digital audio during my undergrad in Sydney, Australia, where I lived for 4 years to study Philosophy & Religion. I began recording and mixing bands in small apartments, learning how to translate my ideas for audio using a computer. Upon my return to Canada I entered full-time work as a FoH sound engineer for a local venue in Victoria. After several years of live audio, I shifted focus to audiovisual media, working on videos and film. This path gradually led me to games, where I have been adapting my audio skills to contribute to building stories for the past two years. I’m self taught, curious, and enjoy collaborating with others towards a creative vision.
