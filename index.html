BIO 
Hi, I'm Nico. I am a sound designer living on Vancouver Island, Canada.

I got my start in digital audio during my undergrad in Sydney, Australia, where I lived for 4 years studying Philosophy & Religion. I started by recording and mixing bands in small apartments, where I began to learn how to translate my ideas using the computer.

On return to Canada I began work as a FoH sound engineer for live music venues. Gradually over the years I worked on more and more audiovisual media, primarily in film post.

My path to better my craft in audio has lead me to games, where I have been using my previous audio experience to build projects in Wwise, Unreal, and Unity.

I am self taught and I enjoy collaborating and problem-solving on technical projects.
