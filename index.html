BIO 
Hi, I'm Nico. I'm a sound designer on Vancouver Island, Canada.

I got my start in digital audio during my undergrad in Sydney, Australia, where I lived for 4 years studying Philosophy & Religion. I was recording and mixing bands in small apartments, where I began to learn how to translate my ideas using the computer.

On return to Canada I began work as a FoH sound engineer for a live music venue. Gradually through the years I began working on more audiovisual media post production for film.

My path in audio would eventually lead me back to games, where I have been using my experience in post production audio to building projects in Wwise, Unreal, and Unity over the past three years.

I am self taught and I enjoy collaborating and problem-solving on technical projects.
